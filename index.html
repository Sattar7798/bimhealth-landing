Features

Everything your BIM team needs

Six powerful tools in one Revit tab. From deep-scan diagnostics to one-click reporting — BIM Health has your model covered.

🔍

9-Category Scanner

Audit your model across Naming Conventions, Revit Warnings, Model Performance, Families & Types, Elements & Geometry, Sheets & Views, Worksets, Linked Models, and Shared Parameters — all in one pass.

⚡

Smart Fix Engine

One-click Auto Fix resolves safe issues instantly. Manual Review mode lets you inspect each issue and approve or skip before any change is made. Every fix is fully undoable with a single Ctrl+Z.

🎯

Live Element Isolation

Click any flagged issue in the results panel and Revit immediately zooms, selects, and isolates the offending element in its native view. No more hunting through 10,000 elements by hand.

📊

Excel + PDF Reports

Generate a 4-sheet Excel workbook (Summary, Issues by Category, Element Detail, Fix Log) and a polished executive PDF summary — ready to attach to handover packages or share with clients.

🔗

BCF 2.1 Export

Push every flagged issue directly to BCF 2.1 format. Compatible with Navisworks, BIM Collaborate Pro, Revizto, and Solibri — so your whole team can track issues regardless of their tool of choice.

📈

Health Score History

BIM Health automatically snapshots your score after every scan. View trend charts over time, see which categories are improving, and demonstrate model quality progress to project stakeholders.

How it works

From open model to clean report in three steps

BIM Health lives inside Revit as a native pyRevit extension — no context-switching, no browser tabs.

1

Open Your Model & Scan

Open any Revit model and click Scan Model. BIM Health inspects all 9 categories in seconds and calculates a 0–100 health score with colour-coded severity flags.

2

Review Issues & Fix

Browse the categorised issue list. Use Auto Fix for safe bulk corrections, or step through Manual Review to approve each change individually.

3

Export Your Report

Click Export Report for a full Excel + PDF package, or Export BCF to push issues to your issue-tracking platform of choice.

FAQ

Common questions

Does BIM Health work with Revit 2022 through 2025?
Yes. BIM Health is tested and fully supported on Revit 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025. Support for new Revit versions is added within 30 days of their official release.
Do I need any other software installed?
Only pyRevit, which is completely free and open-source. Our installer checks for pyRevit automatically and guides you through the setup if it isn't already present. No other dependencies are required.
Is there really a free trial? Do I need a credit card?
Yes — all plans come with a 30-day free trial. No credit card is required to start. You only need to enter payment details if you decide to continue after the trial ends.
Can I undo the changes BIM Health makes?
Absolutely. Every change made by Auto Fix or Manual Review is a standard Revit transaction. A single Ctrl+Z (or Edit → Undo) rolls back all changes from that fix session. You are always in full control.
Does it work with BIM 360 / Autodesk Construction Cloud models?
BIM Health works with locally-opened models today, including those opened from BIM 360 or ACC as a local copy. Native cloud-workshared model support (without a local sync) is on the roadmap and coming in a future release.
How do I get support if something goes wrong?
Email us at support@bimhealth.io. Studio and Office plan customers receive priority response within one business day. A full knowledge base and user guide are available at bimhealth.io/docs.
