Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 January 2026

1. Who We Are

BIM Health ("we", "our", "us") provides Revit model auditing software. Contact: support@bimhealth.io

2. What Data We Collect

Account data: Your email address, collected at purchase via Gumroad.

License data: A machine fingerprint (anonymous hash of hostname + MAC address) used to enforce seat limits. This cannot be used to identify you personally.

Usage events: License activation, validation, and deactivation timestamps. No model data, file names, or Revit content is ever transmitted.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

We never collect, transmit, or store your Revit model data, project files, drawings, or any building information. BIM Health runs entirely locally — only license check calls are made to our server.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your email is used solely to deliver your license key and send renewal reminders. Your machine fingerprint is used solely to enforce seat limits in your subscription plan.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except Gumroad (our payment processor) which operates under its own privacy policy.

6. Data Retention

License and activation data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@bimhealth.io.

7. Security

All data is stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database. License API communications use HTTPS/TLS.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact support@bimhealth.io.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified by email to active subscribers.
